Welcome To My Little Site :)

Hey! Welcome to my little corner of the Web! I'm a massive fan of Web 1.0 (muh nostalgia) and simple, clean websites, so this site is pretty much all that.

I feel websites should be content first, and not try to sell themselves to you with tons of visuals and stuff. There are some sites, however, that blend visuals and content really well (see 'microsounds.github.io')

This site is currently part of a pretty big project of mine, that being Webgoose, a static site generator that will eventually be used to build this site. Currently, Webgoose is non-functional but soon, soon I promise I'll have it working... I just need a few more months....

Who Am I?

I'm Travis (otherwise known as 'Jeff'), a Software Engineering Student and a massive nerd! I'm majorly into software design and development; trains, buses, and all public transport stuffs; audio hardware (mostly headphones); and just building random stuff!
